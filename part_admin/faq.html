IS THE PRODUCT READY STOCK OR I NEED TO PRE-ORDER FIRST?

All of our cases are ready stock and ready to post after we received a new order. For out of stock product, you can contact us through Email or Whatsapp so we can put you on waiting list.

HOW CAN I PROCEED WITH MY PAYMENT?

You can add to cart of your favourite casing and proceed with checkout! Fill in your contact and shipping information and you are ready to make your payment.

CAN I MAKE A CUSTOM WISH CARD?

Yes! We do make Wish Card for any special occasion, you can fill in the wish details section with your own words.

CAN I MAKE A REQUEST FOR INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING?

Yes you can! you can chat with us on the official website and also our customer service number for us to give you the right price for postage fee (only for selected countries). We can arrange the delivery service direct to your door.

DO YOU PROVIDE WORLDWIDE SHIPPING?

As for now, we only ship our products within Malaysia and Singapore. Will make an update if it is available for worldwide shipping soon!

HOW CAN I MAKE CHANGES TO MY ORDER?

You can simply contact us through Whatsapp within 12 hours and your order number! If you want to change your design, please also include the name of the new design.

HOW LONG DOES THE SHIPPING NORMALLY TAKE?

We will ship your product 1-2 days after you succesfully made your payment and the shipping process time depends on your location.

DOES IPHONE 7 THE SAME SIZE AS IPHONE 8?

Yes! Our casing for iphone 7 is also suitable and fit perfectly for iphone 8. Below are the same size cases for iPhone model.

  • Iphone 7 = Iphone 8
  • Iphone 7 Plus = Iphone 8 Plus
  • Iphone X = Iphone XS
  • Iphone 12 = Iphone 12 Pro
